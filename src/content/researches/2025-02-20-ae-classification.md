How can we train AI to read drug labels and instantly warn us about serious health risks like liver or heart damage? In this follow-up study, we applied the AskFDALabel framework directly to real-world drug safety tasks. The system achieved near-perfect accuracy in automatically detecting adverse events and classifying toxicity risks, demonstrating how secure, local AI can speed up pharmacovigilance screening while maintaining 100% data traceability.
